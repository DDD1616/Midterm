DC Comics Feature Films

A Blog by Demetrios Daniel

This is a collection of my three most favourite DC movies that have come out over the years. DC comics have been a great source of inspiration for me over the years so it is amazing that i have the chance to witness those stories come to life on the big screen. Dc has been known for its darker and more mature tone especially when in comparison to its biggest competitor, the Marvel Cinematic Universe. These stories are very important to me since i grew up reading them and have always been particularly fascinated by the way the human psyche is portrayed as well as the exploration of gray areas in our morals which DC comics lays out so well. Please see below for the posters, as well as some information about which year the movie came out as well as who directed it.

The Batman

Batman ventures into Gotham City's underworld when a sadistic killer leaves behind a trail of cryptic clues. As the evidence begins to lead closer to home and the scale of the perpetrator's plans become clear, he must forge new relationships, unmask the culprit and bring justice to the abuse of power and corruption that has long plagued the metropolis.

  • Release Date: March 4, 2022
  • Director: Matt Reeves

    • Posted by Demetrios Daniel on February 23, 2023

    Aquaman

    Once home to the most advanced civilization on Earth, the city of Atlantis is now an underwater kingdom ruled by the power-hungry King Orm. With a vast army at his disposal, Orm plans to conquer the remaining oceanic people -- and then the surface world. Standing in his way is Aquaman, Orm's half-human, half-Atlantean brother and true heir to the throne. With help from royal counselor Vulko, Aquaman must retrieve the legendary Trident of Atlan and embrace his destiny as protector of the deep.

  • Release Date: December 21, 2018
  • Director: James Wan

    • Posted by Demetrios Daniel on February 23, 2023

    The Suicide Squad

    The government sends the most dangerous supervillains in the world -- Bloodsport, Peacemaker, King Shark, Harley Quinn and others -- to the remote, enemy-infused island of Corto Maltese. Armed with high-tech weapons, they trek through the dangerous jungle on a search-and-destroy mission, with only Col. Rick Flag on the ground to make them behave.

  • Release Date: August 06, 2021
  • Director: James Gunn
